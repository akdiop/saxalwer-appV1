Design a complete 3-screen mobile flow for an integrated cultural and health orientation feature in an African women-centered reproductive health app named "SAXALWER".

The feature combines symptom evaluation and cultural context assessment to generate a balanced, personalized orientation.

Style requirements:

Abstract silhouette illustration only

No realistic faces

No photography

Warm sand beige background (#E8DCC8, #F3EADF)

Deep green accents (#0F3D2E)

Copper highlights (#C26A3D)

Cacao brown shadows (#4A2F27)

Subtle circular protection motif

Soft crescent symbol integrated in background

Typography:

Elegant serif headline (Playfair/Cormorant style)

Modern sans serif body text (Inter/Lato style)

The mood must be:
protective, discreet, empowering, culturally grounded, calm.
No clinical blue tones.
No Western hospital aesthetic.

SCREEN 1 — Orientation sensible

Title: "Orientation sensible"
Subtitle: "Quelques questions pour mieux comprendre ta situation."

Generate 6–8 short, culturally sensitive questions combining:

Perception of symptoms

Comfort speaking about the issue

Fear of judgment

Normalization of pain

Access to care

Privacy concerns (shared phone, discretion)

Questions must be gentle and indirect.

Use response scale:
Toujours / Souvent / Parfois / Rarement / Jamais

Include reassurance text:
"Il n’y a pas de bonne ou mauvaise réponse."

SCREEN 2 — Ton orientation personnalisée

Title: "Ton orientation personnalisée"

Display three possible states (create design variants):

Surveillance et conseils

Consultation recommandée

Consultation prioritaire

Tone must be balanced:
Clear but not alarmist.
Reassuring but not minimizing.

Avoid dramatic wording.
Avoid strong warning colors.

Include explanatory paragraph that integrates both medical and contextual factors.

Add subtle line:
"Ton orientation prend en compte plusieurs éléments médicaux et personnels."

Add supportive normalization message:
"Beaucoup de femmes hésitent à consulter par peur du jugement. Tu n’es pas seule."

Add action buttons:

Écouter un audio explicatif

Trouver une professionnelle

SCREEN 3 — Adaptation intelligente

Create a subtle interface state showing that:

Audio recommendations adapt to the user's context

Chatbot tone adapts to emotional and cultural profile

Discreet mode can activate automatically if privacy risk is high

Do NOT show any numerical score.

Show a soft message:
"Nous adaptons les informations à ta situation et à ton contexte."

Use abstract layered shapes to suggest intelligence and personalization.

Keep layout minimal, spacious, calm and elegant.
